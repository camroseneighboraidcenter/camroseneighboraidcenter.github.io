# About Our Food Bank
Our Food Bank Services program has been a cornerstone of our mission to support those in need since 1984. We provide nutritious food supplies to individuals and families experiencing food insecurity in our community.

## Program Goals

- Provide emergency food assistance to those in need
- Ensure access to nutritious and balanced food options
- Support our community members with dignity and respect
- Collaborate with local partners to maximize our impact
  </Section>

<Section type="services">
## Available Services

<ServiceCard title="Food Hamper Program">
- Comprehensive food hampers tailored to household size
- Non-perishable staples (rice, pasta, canned goods)
- Fresh produce when available
- Dairy products and protein options
- Personal care items
</ServiceCard>

<ServiceCard title="Front Room Services">
- Fresh bread and pastries from local bakeries
- Seasonal produce from community gardens
- Soups and beans for quick meals
- Emergency supplies for urgent needs
</ServiceCard>

<Section type="requirements">
## Eligibility & Registration
- Open to all residents of Camrose and surrounding areas
- Valid ID and proof of address required
- No appointment needed for Front Room services
- Registration required for hamper services
- Income assessment for ongoing support
- Accommodation available for mobility needs
</Section>

<Section type="process">
  ## How It Works

1. First Visit

   - Bring ID and proof of address
   - Complete registration (for hampers)
   - Discuss any dietary needs

2. Getting Assistance

   - Front Room: Drop in during hours
   - Hampers: Schedule pickup time
   - Special needs: Arrange accommodation

3. Ongoing Support
   - Regular hamper schedule
   - Access to all services
   - Connection to other programs
</Section>
